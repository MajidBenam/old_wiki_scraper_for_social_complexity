There are very few signs of status differentiation, whether in burials, architecture, or material culture more generally.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_16_ItLatBA][MAJIDBENAM_BR]Population was probably sparse up to the Middle Bronze Age in Italy, with settlements of no more than a few dozen inhabitants each. In contrast, the Late Bronze Age witnessed a significant demographic increase, suggested by an increased number of sites and increased site size.
