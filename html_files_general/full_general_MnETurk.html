♠ Religion Genus ♣ Buddhism ♥ "The religious beliefs of the Türk focused on a sky god, Tängri, and an earth goddess, Umay. Although the Sogdians were a settled, urban people, they were like the Türk in that they also had a Central Eurasian warrior ethos with a pervasive comitatus tradition, and both peoples were intensely interested in trade."
