♠ Capital ♣ Cuzco; Choquepukio ♥ 1000-1300 CE Cuzco became capital of the Killke polity. This has been confirmed by archaeological digs under Cuzco. [MAJIDBENAM_REF_21_PeCuzL1]Choquepukio was the capital of the Lucre Basin (Pinagua) polity. "Choquepukio was the most important settlement in the valley (McEwan 2006). Occupied by the Pinahua, it was quite an impressive town and may have even approached Killke-era Cuzco in scale and grandeur. Its ruins cover 60 ha on a low ridge and boast architecture bordering on the monumental - large stone enclosures and multi-storey buildings." [MAJIDBENAM_REF_22_PeCuzL1][MAJIDBENAM_BR]Language
