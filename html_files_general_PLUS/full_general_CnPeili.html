♠ Religion Sect ♣ ♥The Peiligang culture existed from 7000 to 5000 BC in the Yi-Luo river basin of the Middle Yellow River Valley (modern Henan Province, China). There have been over 100 sites along river banks identified with this Neolithic culture.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_1_CnPeili][MAJIDBENAM_BR]Archaeologists have found bone and stone tools and ceramics from the period as well as weapons including harpoons with bone points, stone spears, and arrows which suggests evidence of the use of bows during this time.[MAJIDBENAM_BR]The people of the Peiligang culture hunted deer and wild boar and fished using nets.
