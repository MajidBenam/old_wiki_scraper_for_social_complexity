The period is also known as the Onin War and the Age of the Warring States (which translated into Japanese becomes the Sengoku jidai or Sengoku Period).[MAJIDBENAM_REF_7_JpSengk][MAJIDBENAM_BR]There was no central government. At times the daimyo made alliances with each other in the quest for more power.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_8_JpSengk][MAJIDBENAM_BR]Despite the turmoil the population during this period probably increased by five million over 100 years to about 20 million in 1568 CE.
