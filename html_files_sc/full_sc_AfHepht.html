Islamic sources describe a series of disastrous campaigns by the Sassasian King Peroz that resulted in his eventual death.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_31_AfHepht] Following this victory, the Hepthalties secured control over Central Asia, Korasan and Afganistan from the Sassanian and Kushan Kingdoms.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_32_AfHepht] They continued to expand until the sixth century, when they were defeated by a recovered Sassasian Empire fighting alongside another confederation of central Asian nomads known as the Gokturks.
