Bureaucracy characteristics

♠ Full-time bureaucrats ♣ inferred absent ♥ "However, it would be incorrect to consider these innovations as evidence of the emergence among the Rouran of a bureaucratic organisation of administration resembling the Chinese one. Most likely, it was only an outward similarity with Chinese institutions, its main aim was the preservation of steppe traditions.
