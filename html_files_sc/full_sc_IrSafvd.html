Bureaucracy characteristics

The central government employed full-time bureaucratic officials, including chief accountants, comptroller-general and chief scribe. They were paid a salary, although it was not a meritocratic system: some post were hereditary and others picked by the Shah. The government did have its own specialist buildings such as the mints..
