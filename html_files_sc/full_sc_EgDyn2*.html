♠ markets ♣ ♥ Before the New Kingdom inter-regional trade was conducted between institutions. "Merchants who worked for their own gain existed in ancient Egypt only during the New Kingdom." Non-consumables included household artifacts.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_56_EgDyn2*] However: "non-institutional trade networks should be considered.
