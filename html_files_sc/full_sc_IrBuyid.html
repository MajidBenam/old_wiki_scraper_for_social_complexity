♠ Professional military officers ♣ present ♥A "military aristocracy" grew out of the success of the Dalamite conquest.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_78_IrBuyid] [MAJIDBENAM_REF_79_IrBuyid][MAJIDBENAM_BR]"The iqta had been the Buyid answer to the problem of paying the military in a period when specie was in short supply: in lieu of salary an amir would be granted the right to collect the taxes of a given area. In principle, the iqta remained in the figt of the sultan, and could be withdrawn at any moment.
