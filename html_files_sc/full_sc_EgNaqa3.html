♠ markets ♣ inferred present ♥ Before the New Kingdom inter-regional trade was conducted between institutions. Goods exchanged at markets were primarily consumables like beer and bread, also some dried meat, fish, vegetables and fruits. Non-consumables included household artifacts.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_67_EgNaqa3] However: "non-institutional trade networks should be considered.
