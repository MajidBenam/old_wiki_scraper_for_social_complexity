Bureaucracy characteristics

Outside of the Khan's royal household, the Mongols acquired a bureaucratic apparatus as they conquered sedentary regions. This meant that was some diversity in bureaucratic practice across the empire with local written languages being used e.g. Persian, Chinese. The Chinese examinations system was eventually revived under the Mongol's although not used elsewhere. [MAJIDBENAM_REF_38_MnMngKh]
