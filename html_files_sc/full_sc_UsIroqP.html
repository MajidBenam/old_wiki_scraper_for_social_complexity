Hierarchical Complexity

♠ Settlement hierarchy ♣ 2 ♥ levels. At the first level were semisedentary villages that the Iroquois lived in year round, but moved to new village sites every 10 to 25 years. The second level consisted of 1 to 4 villages that were part of tribal clusters, which could include chiefs in certain areas.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_5_UsIroqP]
