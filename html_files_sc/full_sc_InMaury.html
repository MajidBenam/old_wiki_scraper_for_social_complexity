♠ Full-time bureaucrats ♣ present ♥Large "tentacular" bureaucracy.[MAJIDBENAM_REF_93_InMaury][MAJIDBENAM_BR]The King ruled with the help of a small permanent staff of elder statesmen called the Mantriparisad.
